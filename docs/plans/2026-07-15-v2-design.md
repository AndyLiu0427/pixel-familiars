# Pixel Familiars v2 - Fusion, Collection, Equipment, Art, Launch

Date: 2026-07-15
Basis: three parallel research passes (idle systems design, pixel art technique,
browser game distribution). Decisions below adapt those findings to this game.

## 1. Fusion: shard-based star-up (合成進化)

One pet instance per species. Duplicate hatches auto-convert to that species'
shards. Rationale: AFK Arena-style escalating dupe costs sustain pulls for
months; auto-shard (Idle Heroes/Archero pattern) makes every pull a visible
increment and needs zero inventory UI, right-sized for 11 species.

- Star costs: 2 / 3 / 5 / 8 / 12 shards (0★ to 5★, 30 total)
- Shards per dupe by hatched rarity: 1 / 2 / 4 / 8 / 16
- Each star: +25% base stats, +10 level cap (cap = 30 + 10 x stars)
- Hatching a higher rarity than owned upgrades the pet's rarity (old value
  refunds shards); overflow shards past 5★ auto-convert 5 shards = 1 gem
- Pity: guaranteed legendary within 40 eggs; unowned species hatch at 2x
  weight until the book is filled

## 2. Equipment (裝備): 3 slots, one currency, one button

Nonstop Knight's 3-slot + auto-equip is the reference for menu-free idle UX.
Equipment is the steady AFK drip; gacha stays the dominant power axis.

- Slots: weapon (flat ATK), armor (flat HP), charm (one % substat:
  ATK% / HP% / gold%)
- Rarities: common / rare / epic / legendary (mult 1 / 1.5 / 2.25 / 3.4)
- Drops: mobs 4% (common:rare = 3:1); zone bosses 100%, epic possible from
  zone 3, legendary from zone 8
- Item stat = 5 x (dropZone + 1) x rarityMult; forge +8%/level, 10 levels,
  cost 10 x 1.4^level dust; salvage below epic gives dust (1 / 3)
- Inventory cap 50, auto-salvage lowest on overflow; buttons: 一鍵最強裝備,
  分解低階裝備
- Budget rule: fully equipped ≈ 1.5x naked power

## 3. Collection book (圖鑑): 22 entries, permanent power

Idleon cards / Pokéclicker pattern: collection IS power, silhouettes convert
unknowns into goals.

- 11 species x 2 variants (normal + shiny 1/100 hatch) = 22 entries
- States: silhouette → revealed → mastered (5★)
- Permanent bonuses: +2% team ATK and HP per revealed entry, +3% more per
  mastered species
- Milestones: 5 / 10 / 15 / 20 entries → 100 gems / free egg / secret 11th
  species (虛空龍 Voidwyrm) / golden frame
- Shiny = alternate palette + sparkle particles

## 4. Art upgrade (美術)

Per research: 16x16 cannot hold face + limbs + shading. All creatures
re-authored at 24x24 with the Apollo palette (Lospec), selective outlining,
hue-shifted 3-5 color ramps, one top-left light source. Animation is
programmatic (research: Pokémon Crystal shipped 2-frame idles): breathing =
top rows shift 1px on alternate beat; hit = white flash + 2px knockback;
no drawn frames.

## 5. Retention + launch (from distribution research)

In this build: 7-day daily reward streak (strongest cheap retention lever).
Post-build checklist (docs/plans/launch-checklist, summarized):

1. Own domain + AdSense (H5 ads only work there; github.io cannot verify)
2. Content pages (how-to-play, pet list, FAQ) so AdSense sees a real site
3. r/incremental_games Feedback Friday → release post; 巴哈姆特獨立遊戲板 +
   Dcard 遊戲板 (dev-story format); galaxy.click / incrementaldb listings
4. Portals: full game on CrazyGames (their SDK), Poki pitch, Newgrounds,
   itch.io devlog. No lite-funnel (banned); brand + save export is the bridge
5. Later: share cards on rare pulls, long-tail SEO pages per pet
